Rethink the default

The traditional retirement model is broken.

Accounts alone are not a plan. Market losses near retirement can permanently reduce future income, while taxes can quietly consume more of every withdrawal. A coordinated strategy organizes growth, risk, taxes, income, and legacy before the transition begins.

The traditional model

Growth exposed to two unknowns.

Market volatility can reduce the account when income is needed most, while future tax rates determine how much of the balance you actually keep.

  • Sequence-of-returns risk
  • Unpredictable future tax drag
  • No coordinated income floor

The Watts framework

Protection creates room to plan.

We evaluate strategies designed to prevent market losses from reducing principal, while coordinating tax treatment and dependable income options.

  • Strict 0% market floor on applicable strategies
  • Principal protected from negative index performance
  • Income coordinated around your timeline
