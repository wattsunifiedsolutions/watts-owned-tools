    Section 02

    Growth Engine & Architecture

    02/05

    The Power of the 0% Floor & Engineering

    In an Indexed Universal Life (IUL) structure, your cash value is linked to a market index. When the market goes down, your cash value is protected by a 0% floor. We structure the policy with the lowest possible death benefit required by the IRS and push the premium payments to the absolute limit of the "MEC Line", ensuring the policy stays a non-taxable asset while absorbing as much cash as possible.
