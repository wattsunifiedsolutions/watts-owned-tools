Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common questions about evaluating transition, insurance, and retirement options.

How do I evaluate my TSP rollover options?

Start by reviewing your current allocations, fees, time horizon, income needs, and risk tolerance. A review can compare keeping assets in the TSP with other available options. A rollover is not automatically appropriate, and every alternative should be evaluated carefully.

What should I know about VGLI premium increases?

VGLI uses age-banded rates that generally increase as you move into a new band. Its guaranteed-acceptance window can be valuable, while long-term affordability may lead some veterans to compare other coverage for which underwriting and eligibility apply.

What does a personalized review include?

It is an educational conversation about your military background, current coverage, retirement accounts, family needs, and long-term goals. The purpose is to identify questions, potential gaps, and options that may deserve a closer look.

Is there an obligation after the review?

No. The goal is to give you useful information so you can decide what fits. There is no obligation to implement any strategy discussed.
